Visit Ankara

As you wander through the historic streets of Ankara, you will take a journey between the past and the future in this city where the heart of Turkey beats.

Top three activities to do at Ankara

Visit Anıtkabir

Anıtkabir, where the tomb of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, the founder of Turkey, is located, is one of the most important symbols of Ankara. Here you can visit Atatürk's mausoleum and museum and express your admiration and respect for Turkish history.

Visit ODTÜ

While visiting the campus, you can see buildings with modern architecture, green areas and ponds. You can also explore the METU Library, interact with students and participate in the university's cultural events. METU hosts many different clubs, events and exhibitions in the fields of science, technology and arts.

Explore Ankara Castle

Ankara Castle is a magnificent spot offering panoramic views of the city. You can visit the castle, explore historical buildings and museums, and taste local delicacies in the surrounding streets.
